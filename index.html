A six-letter word puzzle.

Topsy Wordy is a word puzzle for iPhone. Each puzzle gives you six letters. Find the word they make, then build it backwards.

The first 50 puzzles are free. A one-time purchase unlocks the full collection of 1,000 puzzles. The app has no account, subscription, advertising, or tracking.

The app works fully offline. The puzzle library is bundled in the app.
