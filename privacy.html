Privacy Notice

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Topsy Wordy is designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

1. Information stored on your device

The app stores information locally on your device, such as:

  • which puzzles you have solved, your best star rating for each, and your best flawless run
  • the local order in which this install offers the puzzle collection
  • the committed state of your current puzzle, so it can reopen where you left it
  • whether you have completed the how-to-play tutorial
  • whether you have accepted the in-app legal gate
  • your puzzles-per-session choice
  • your reminder preference and the time you chose
  • local counters and dates used to decide whether the app may ask Apple to show the standard App Store review prompt
  • a record of the on-device storage version

This information is used to make the app work and to remember your progress and settings. It stays on your device.

2. Reminders

If you enable the daily reminder, the app uses your device's local notification system to schedule a single repeating reminder at the time you choose. The reminder is scheduled on your device and is not sent from, or recorded by, any server.

You can turn the reminder off in the app or through your device settings.

3. Analytics, tracking, and network use

Topsy Wordy does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cross-app tracking. The game, puzzle data, progress, reminders, sharing text, and settings do not use a developer server, and the app does not send your puzzle progress or settings to us.

If the app asks Apple to show the standard App Store review prompt, that request is handled by Apple's StoreKit system. The app does not receive or store whether you rated or reviewed it.

If analytics are added in a future version, this Privacy Notice will be updated before release.

4. Data sharing

The app does not collect or sell personal data. Personal data contained in any correspondence you send to us is processed solely to handle and respond to that correspondence, and is retained only for as long as is necessary for that purpose.

We do not share your progress or app data with advertisers.

5. App Store data

If you download or purchase the app through the Apple App Store, Apple may process information relating to your download, purchase, device, and account under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

6. Support enquiries

If you contact us for support, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, screenshots, device details, or any other details you include.

We use this information only to respond to your enquiry, manage support, and keep appropriate records. Our lawful basis for this processing is our legitimate interest in responding to support enquiries, managing support, and keeping appropriate records.

7. Data retention

Local app data remains on your device unless you delete it, reset the app, delete the app, or your device removes it.

Support enquiries may be retained for a reasonable period to manage support and records.

8. Your rights

Topsy Wordy does not collect or hold personal data about you, beyond what you choose to send if you contact us for support.

With respect to data handling, you have a statutory right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) or your local data protection authority.

9. Data controller and contact

Topsy Wordy is published by Considered Ideas Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 17258515) with its registered office at 66 Paul Street, London EC2A 4NA. Considered Ideas Ltd is the data controller for any personal data described in this notice. For any questions about how your information is used, please write to enquiries@consideredideas.com.
