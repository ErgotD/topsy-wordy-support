FAQ and Support

A short collection of answers to common questions.

How do I play?

Look for the six-letter word in the jumble. Then build it backwards: place the last letter first, then the next-to-last letter, and keep working back until the locked first letter slots into place.

Why was my letter not accepted?

The next letter must match the next position in the backwards build. If a letter bounces back, it is not the next letter the current word needs.

Where can I see the rules again?

Open Settings, then choose How to play.

How do reminders work?

The daily reminder is optional and off by default. If you enable it, iOS schedules one local reminder at the time you choose. You can change or turn off the reminder in Settings.

What is included for free?

The first 50 puzzles are fully playable for free. A one-time purchase unlocks the full collection of 1,000 puzzles. There is no subscription.

Can I restore my purchase?

Yes. Open Settings, tap Unlock Full Game, then tap Restore Purchases. If Apple verifies your purchase, Topsy Wordy unlocks the full game on this device so it remains playable offline.

Does the app need an internet connection?

No. Every puzzle ships inside the app. Topsy Wordy works in aeroplane mode and never makes a network request during normal play. Buying or restoring the full game uses Apple's in-app purchase system and needs the App Store to be available.

Does the app collect any data about me?

No. Your progress, settings, reminder preference, and current puzzle state live only on your device. The Privacy Notice covers this in full.

I found a problem, or I have feedback. How do I tell you?

If a puzzle looks incorrect or you would like to suggest an improvement, please get in touch using the contact details below.
